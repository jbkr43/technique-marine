FAQs

Are you insured?

Yes. Technique Marine is fully licensed and marine-insured for both mobile repair operations and professional captain/vessel delivery services, protecting your investment at the dock and underway.

My boat is at a dry-stack facility or marina. Can you work there?

In most cases, yes. We regularly coordinate with local Cape Fear marinas and dry-storage facilities. As long as the facility allows outside insured vendors, we can work right on-site.

Do you travel outside of the Cape Fear region?

Our standard service area is one hour from our home base in south Wilmington. We are happy to service outside of that range with relative travel time added. Please call for an estimated additional cost.
